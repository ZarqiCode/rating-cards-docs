# Business model

**Audience:** Founders, sales, AI assistants.

## Physical products

Sold as **one-time purchases**. No subscription, no commitment. A venue can buy a single card, try it on one table, and see results before going further. Every purchase includes **device setup by the rating.cards team** — devices arrive configured and live.

Product types: cards, stickers, stands, posters. See [product-capabilities.md](./product-capabilities.md).

## Autopilot subscription

**$20/month**, flat **per account in v1** — not priced per storefront. Unlocks:

- AI-drafted responses (where enabled per verified storefront)
- SMS notification bot per verified manager line
- Weekly pulse email

Physical products are sold separately.

**Per-location Stripe quantity** and advanced commercial packaging are explicitly **deferred v1**. See [multi-location.md](./multi-location.md).

## GTM model

**Sales-led.** Antonio drives outreach, demos, hardware sales, and Autopilot upsell. Typical path in [customer-journey.md](./customer-journey.md).

## Expansion revenue

- More physical devices per venue or across locations
- Autopilot retention after hardware proves value
- Multi-store operators: one account, multiple storefronts, flat subscription in v1
